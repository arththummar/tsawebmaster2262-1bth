Our Preparation Process

Discover the artistry behind every dish at The Green Canvas. From farm-fresh ingredients to masterful plating, our process is a journey of flavor and sustainability.

Click the timeline or dots below to spotlight each step of our eco-friendly preparation.

Fresh Harvest

We scour local farms for the ripest veggies and fruits, hand-picked to lock in that farm-to-fork freshness.

Pure Cleanse

Every leaf and root gets a deep cleanse, stripping away impurities while keeping the good stuff intact.

Precision Cuts

Our chefs wield blades like artists, slicing with precision to ensure every bite cooks and looks just right.

Flavor Infusion

We soak our ingredients in bold marinades, letting spices and herbs weave magic into every fiber.

Artful Cooking

From sizzling pans to gentle steams, we cook with eco-smart methods to amplify taste and nutrition.

Masterful Plating

We craft each plate like a canvas, blending colors and textures into a feast for your eyes and soul.
